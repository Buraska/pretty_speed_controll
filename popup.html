Video Speed Toggle

Quick guide

  1. Hold the click on video to toggle speed rate between 1.0x and custom speed
  2. Put mouse up under the lock icon to lock the speed.
  3. Click the lock-circle icon to open speed toggle controller.
  4. Holding the click on doesn’t just make the video faster - it toggles between 1.0x and your chosen speed. To switch between direction, press the ⇆ button on the controller.
